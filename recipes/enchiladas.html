Green Enchiladas

Home



Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups Monterry Jack or Chihuahua cheese, shredded
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • For the sauce:
    • 1 lb tomatillos, husks removed and rinsed
    • 3-4 serrano peppers (adjust to your spicy preference)
    • ¼ onion
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • ¼ cup fresh cilantro
    • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. To make the sauce, boil the tomatillos, serrano peppers, onion, and garlic until tender. Blend with the cilantro and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and lightly fry each tortilla until soft, then dip it in the sauce.
  3. Fill each tortilla with shredded chicken and a sprinkle of cheese. Roll them up and place them in a baking dish.
  4. Pour the rest of the sauce over the enchiladas, top with the remaining cheese and a drizzle of sour cream.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly
